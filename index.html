Starling

Step 1

Point it at any screen.

Starling analyzes your current design and gives you honest, specific feedback. What's working, what's not, and why users are struggling.

Step 2

Five directions, with feedback on each.

Starling generates five distinct variations of your screen — not random noise, but real design directions. Each one gets synthetic user feedback so you know what's landing.

Step 3

Iterate until something great shakes out.

Combine the best elements. Kill the rest. Run another round. Starling narrows five directions down to one — all inside Figma, as a clickable prototype you can actually feel.
