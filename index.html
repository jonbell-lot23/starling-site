Starling

Step 1

Analyse.

We start with your existing screen. Got real user feedback? Great, we'll use it. If not, synthetic users fill the gap. Either way, you get an honest read on what's working and what's not.

Step 2

Swarm.

Five directions, not five random guesses. Each one gets feedback from synthetic users who are brutally honest about what works. You get signal, not noise.

Step 3

Iterate.

Keep the good stuff, kill the rest, run it again. Five becomes three becomes one. You end up with a Figma prototype you can click through and actually feel.
