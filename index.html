The Problem

Think small.

That's the message designers keep hearing. Your product can be better — your users know it too.

The gap between what you ship and what you could ship keeps growing.

How It Works

Six steps from stale to stellar.

01
Baseline report
Starling analyzes the current screen and tells you why it's struggling.
02
Divergent exploration
Five distinct directions. Three screens each. All within your design system.
03
Synthetic feedback
AI users score each direction with specific, actionable quotes.
04
Feel it move
Click into each variation. Experience the interactions firsthand.
05
Combine & iterate
Cherry-pick the best elements. Remix. Run the next round.
06
Converge
Five becomes three, becomes two, becomes one. The best design wins.
